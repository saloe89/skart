1. Introduction

Welcome to Skart. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Skart mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Skart is developed and maintained by Sascha ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our"). If you have questions, contact us at saschinka196@gmail.com.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely transparent: Skart does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

Your name, email address, or any contact information

Location data

Device identifiers or advertising IDs

Usage analytics or crash reports

Any data from your shopping lists

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into Skart (such as shopping list items) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data:

Never leaves your device

Is not accessible to the Developer or any third party

Is deleted when you uninstall the App or clear the App's data

4. Third-Party Services

Skart does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

5. Children's Privacy

Skart does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no data at all, this App is safe for all ages.

Since no personal data is processed, no data protection obligations arise under either framework.
